A Bird's Eye View

A Bird’s Eye View is a short 16 mm animated film in which a man sends a rainbow-winged bird far beyond the clouds on an unusual adventure.

Produced, directed and animated by Deborah Short

Piano adaptation of Chopin’s Waltz in E flat opus 18 by Ethel Wadsworth

Score, synthesizer, sound effects and mix by Loren Jones

Honors:

  • 1981 Winner Academy Award for Animation
  • 1981 Finalist Short Film Showcase
  • 1982 Third Place Focus Award for Animation
  • 1982 Sinking Creek Film Festival Award for Animation
  • 1982 Honorable Mention for Animation - Baltimore International Film Festival
  • 1982 Cine Eagle Award
  • 1982 Wilbert H. Pearson Memorial Award Presented by Cine to a Film Furthering International Human Understanding Through a Non-verbal Mode
